# Escape From Yerba Buena

*Escape From Yerba Buena* is a 7 Day Roguelike jam entry: a turn-based western set in 1850s San Francisco, built with [Bevy](https://bevyengine.org/) and [bevy_ratatui](https://github.com/ratatui/bevy_ratatui). The game drops you into Yerba Buena just after a riverfront jail cell blows open, while four gangs are already fighting for control of the city.

The objective is to make it out alive by reaching any edge of town. Along the way, you explore a procedurally generated frontier city, scavenge for weapons and supplies, manage your ammo and stamina, and try not to get caught in the middle while the factions tear into each other. It’s a desperate escape more than a heroic cleanup job, so staying mobile and choosing your fights carefully matters.
