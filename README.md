# Escape From Yerba Buena

*Escape From Yerba Buena* is a 7 Day Roguelike jam entry: a turn-based 1850s western built with [Bevy](https://bevyengine.org/) and [bevy_ratatui](https://github.com/ratatui/bevy_ratatui). You start in Yerba Buena after a riverfront jail cell explodes open, and four gangs are already tearing the city to pieces around you.

Your goal is simple: reach any edge of Yerba Buena alive. On the way out, you move through a procedurally generated frontier town, scavenge weapons and supplies, manage ammo and stamina, and try to stay ahead of the chaos while rival factions fight each other in the streets. It’s a run-for-your-life western where staying mobile, picking your fights, and grabbing what you need matters more than trying to clean up the whole town.
